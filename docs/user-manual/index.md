# VNM Direct Drive Wheelbase Specifications

VNM Direct Drive Wheelbase Family (abbreviated as: "wheelbase") has 4 types which have different specifications as bellow

|                                   | Premier                     | Elite                    | Supreme                  | Xtreme                   |

| SKU                               | VNM-DD01PRE                 | VNM-DD01ELI              | VNM-DD01SUP              | VNM-DD01XTR              |

| Motor                             | 5 polepairs smooth motor    | 5 polepairs smooth motor | 5 polepairs smooth motor | 5 polepairs smooth motor |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| Encoder                           | 23-bit encoder              | 23-bit encoder           | 23-bit encoder           | 23-bit encoder           |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| Maximum torque                    | 13Nm                        | 18Nm                     | 25Nm                     | 32Nm                     |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| VNM QR Base Side                  | Included                    | Included                 | Included                 | Included                 |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+--------------------------+
