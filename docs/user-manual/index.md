# VNM Direct Drive Wheelbase Specifications

VNM Direct Drive Wheelbase Family (abbreviated as: "wheelbase") has 4 types which have different specifications as below.

***Please read the manual carefully before installing or operating the wheelbases.***

---

## Model Comparison

| | **Premier** | **Elite** | **Supreme** | **Xtreme** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **SKU** | VNM-DD01PRE | VNM-DD01ELI | VNM-DD01SUP | VNM-DD01XTR |
| **Motor** | 5 pole pairs smooth motor | 5 pole pairs smooth motor | 5 pole pairs smooth motor | 5 pole pairs smooth motor |
| **Encoder** | 23-bit | 23-bit | 23-bit | 23-bit |
| **Maximum torque** | 13 Nm | 18 Nm | 25 Nm | 32 Nm |
| **VNM QR Base Side** | Included | Included | Included | Included |
| **EMC Stop Button** | Regular (Superior optional) | Regular (Superior optional) | Superior | Superior |
| **Power supply** | 300 W | 360 W | 480 W | 480 W |
| **Dimensions** (without QR) | 130×130×228 mm | 130×130×247 mm | 130×130×267 mm | 130×130×271 mm |
| **Weight** | 11.6 kg | 13.3 kg | 15.7 kg | 16 kg |
| **Packing box** | 47×35×24 cm | 47×35×24 cm | 49×35×24 cm | 49×35×24 cm |

---

## Box Contents

Each wheelbase comes with:

- Wheelbase
- Power Supply
- USB A to B cable
- USB A to C cable
- EMC Stop Button
- VNM Quick Release Base Side
- 4× M8 bolts
- Hex wrench set
